Firm Grip Security Consult Ltd is a world-class provider of security services tailored to meet the needs of financial institutions, high-risk industries, as well as mining. We have built a reputation for excellence, professionalism, and reliability by consistently delivering high-level security solutions.

We are passionate about helping financial institutions and mining organizations as well as individual miners achieve their goals by providing expert security guidance, support, and solutions that address their unique challenges and risks, fostering a culture of security excellence and business success. 'Firm Grip' also provides general capacity building in leadership, strategy, and the soft skills to the corporate world.

Our Mission & Vision

At Firm Grip Security Consult, our mission is to provide tailored security consulting services to the financial and mining sectors, leveraging our expertise to ensure full compliance, mitigate risks and promote a safe, secure and prosperous business environment.

Our vision is to be the 'go-to' in the consulting industry by providing innovative, effective and sustainable solutions that drive business growth, mitigate risks and foster a culture of security awareness and best practices
